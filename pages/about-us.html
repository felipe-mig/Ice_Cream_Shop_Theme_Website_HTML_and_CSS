ABOUT US

We dreamed of creating the most unbelievably delicious ice cream.

It came out of the blue, but it made sense. We loved a scoop or two of the good stuff and had often talked about making something with our own two hands; a real product for real people.

He set to work in his small barn in Gold Coast. On a cold winters day in December 2002 we walked across the field to the village pub and sold the very first tub of ICE CREAM.

MISSION

We were soon working with top chefs around the country, designing new flavours for their menus.

Making our ice cream with the traditional batch method, we carefully select the best ingredients; for example, single estate Colombian cacao, Madagascan bourbon vanilla and fresh milk and cream.

We've tasted and tested literally thousands of recipes over the years and won over 70 Great Taste Awards.

VISION

We are fortunate to live and work in an incredibly beautiful corner of our incredibly beautiful planet. The rolling hills of Gold Coast remind us daily that we have a responsibility to protect our natural environment.

We have teamed up with one of the world's leading experts in carbon footprinting to make sure that ICE CREAM is reducing carbon intensity as fast as possible. We have a target of a 43% reduction by 2030 from our 2020 base, and have achieved a 21% reduction so far.

VALUES

We believe that all businesses should benefit people and communities.

ICE CREAM has been a team effort from day one with family, friends and neighbours working together. We've learnt a lot along the way and, after 20 years, we're proud to have a team of the best ice cream makers in the world.
